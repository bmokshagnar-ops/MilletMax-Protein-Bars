India's Health Challenge

Most of us want to eat healthy, but nutrition is often either too expensive or too unhealthy.

Too Expensive

Most protein bars in the market cost ₹150 or more, and they're filled with imported ingredients, making health a luxury.

Too Unhealthy

Cheaper snacks are often loaded with sugar, preservatives, and processed ingredients, offering little to no real nutritional value.

Our Solution: India's Superfood

We wanted to change that. So we created MilletMax, a tasty, affordable protein bar made from India’s own superfood — millets!

India is the largest producer of millets like ragi, jowar, and bajra — ancient grains rich in protein, fiber, and iron.

We combined these grains with peanuts, jaggery, honey, and dry fruits to make a bar that’s truly wholesome.

Packed with Real Energy

  • ✅ 100% Natural: No artificial flavors or sweeteners.
  • ✅ Free from Preservatives: Just wholesome, natural ingredients.
  • ✅ Packed with Goodness: Each bar gives 10–12 grams of protein.

It's enough to power your day, whether you’re a student, athlete, or just someone who wants to eat right.
